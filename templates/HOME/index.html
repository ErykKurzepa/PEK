Hey There!

I'm Eryk, an experienced engineer with passion for mechanical engineering, manufacturing, automatic control and robotics, computer science and internet technology.

About Me

Since I can remember, I have always been interested in computers and mathematics. Starting from High School where my class profile was mathematics and computer science I was amazed with possibilities that computer programming can provide in solving mathematical challenges. My first PC was home build Intel Celeron 300MHz and 128 MB RAM memory, and that was all I needed to build some simple PC apps in Turbo Pascal - a programming language that probably some youngest programmer doesn't even know it existed! :)

Many years have passed, and I have a different PC now. I also have a loving, beautiful, smart, sexy, amazing wife Karolina, 3 children: Leon, Emil and Karol and a dog Ella.

One thing didn't change though: My passion to mathematics and computers!

My Education and Skills

After completing my high school, I continued my education at Kielce University of Technology (Poland) where I have acquired two university degrees:

  • Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering (year 2007)
  • Master's degree in Applied Computer Science (year 2008)
At the university I gained some basic knowledge about web development using HTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL. I also learned programming languages: PHP, Delphi, Visual Basic for Excel. I have also become fascinated with mechanical engineering, especially computer aided designing CAD, computer aided manufacturing CAM and Computer Numerical Controlled (CNC) machines.

After completing my university education I have chosen the mechanical engineering to be my professional career patch. Over the years I have gained broad experience in designing and manufacturing of mechanical components. I have gained a lot of experience with CAD and CAM software, especially Catia and NX, but also Mastercam and Powermill. I have been testing and creating solutions using some great NX components like: Product Template Studio, Machining Knowledge Editor, PostBuilder. The amount of automation and optimization of CAM programming that NX is offering is just wonderful.

In recent years I became more and more interested in Computer Science, Internet of Things, Automatic Control and Robotics. I started to use my computer science education and acquired IT skills whenever I had possibility to optimize my mechanical engineering work. I have learned basics of C#, ASP.NET,MSSQL Server and Visual Studio. Recently I have also learned Python as it seems to be very flexible language with a wide variety of usage. I have also learned Django framework, and that's what I used for creating this web page. I use IT knowledge to build small computer programs that help me with my daily work as mechanical engineer. You can see few examples of such programs / software on this web page, check my CAD, CAM and IT pages for more details.

My other passion is Automatic Control and Robotics. Few years ago I started learning about electronics, home automation, sensors, controlling IoT project using API technology etc. Arduino (open-source electronics platform) became my hobby and I started completing different Arduino projects (toy cars, android apps for controlling lights with Bluetooth etc) - check my IoT page for details. This hobby evaluated into something more serious: in 2019 I completed postgraduate studies: Industrial Control Systems in AGH University of Science and Technology in Krakow (Poland)

This site is a collection of my work, interests, and writings; if any of those things intrigue you, don't hesitate to reach out.
